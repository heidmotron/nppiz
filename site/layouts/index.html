Protect Medical Workers from the Coronavirus

Intubating doctors can be exposed to high viral loads from a breathing or coughing COVID-19 patient (NEJM, 2020).

By providing a Negative Pressure Patient Isolation Zone (NPPIZ) that can be quickly and cheaply deployed, we at Chamber 19 aim to reduce high viral load exposure for doctors and nurses.

How it works?

The Chamber 19 kit includes:

  • Aerosol Box or Oxygen Tent
  • miniSQUAIR bioaerosol capture device

What you'll need:

  • Smoke evacuation system
  • Self-adhesive plastic sheeting (if using Aerosol Box)
